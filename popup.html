info_outline User Guide (Critical Steps)
Welcome to AlloChat! Please complete these critical steps, otherwise the application will not function properly:
monetization_on
1. ALGO Funds (Message Fees) Every message is an E2E microtransaction on the Algorand blockchain. You must have a minimum amount of ALGO on your address. Each message fee is less than 0.001 ALGO. Top up your wallet by clicking your balance in the app header.
cloud_upload
2. Images & Media (Pinata IPFS) If you want to send image attachments, you must configure your Pinata IPFS JWT Token in settings. Otherwise, file sharing is disabled to prevent unnecessary fees. Images are automatically encrypted before uploading!
key
3. Backup Recovery SEED! Your private keys are generated from a 25-word recovery phrase (SEED). Save them in a secure, physical location! If you lose your SEED or forget your passcode, you will completely lose access to your account and contacts.
